About the Firm

Boutique expertise.
International reach.

Astra Solutions is a dynamically developing boutique law firm providing comprehensive legal services in intellectual property, business consulting, and dispute resolution.

Our team combines deep local expertise with international experience, advising clients across Kazakhstan and nine neighbouring jurisdictions — from Kyrgyzstan and Uzbekistan to Georgia and Mongolia.

We deliver tailored, commercially-minded advice that protects and grows what matters most: the ideas, assets, and ambitions of our clients.
